CapWise

People Counting and Analysis

Every Customer Counts

CapWise is an open-source, mobile-friendly application to collect and make good use of occupancy data. Our design team has created this application with the goal of keeping analytics simple and easy to use for business-ownders of all sizes. Click log-in to get started today and sign up!
